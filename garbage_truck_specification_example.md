### PROJECT OVERVIEW
Blacktown City Council is seeking to procure 5 x side loading garbage trucks to replace aging waste collection vehicles. The vehicles will be used for residential waste collection, recycling collection, and green waste collection across the Blacktown Local Government Area.

### SCOPE OF WORK
The successful tenderer shall supply and deliver 5 x side loading garbage trucks that meet all specified requirements, including:

1. **Vehicle Supply**: 5 x new side loading garbage trucks
2. **Delivery**: Delivery to Blacktown City Council waste depot
3. **Training**: Operator training for 10 personnel (2 per vehicle)
4. **Warranty**: 5-year comprehensive warranty
5. **Support**: 36-month parts and service support
6. **Documentation**: Complete technical documentation and manuals
