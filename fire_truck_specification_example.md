### PROJECT OVERVIEW
Blacktown City Council is seeking to procure 2 x heavy duty fire trucks to replace aging emergency response vehicles. The vehicles will be used for fire suppression, rescue operations, and emergency response across the Blacktown Local Government Area.

### SCOPE OF WORK
The successful tenderer shall supply and deliver 2 x heavy duty fire trucks that meet all specified requirements, including:

1. **Vehicle Supply**: 2 x new heavy duty fire trucks
2. **Delivery**: Delivery to Blacktown City Council fire station
3. **Training**: Operator training for 12 personnel (6 per vehicle)
4. **Warranty**: 7-year comprehensive warranty
5. **Support**: 48-month parts and service support
6. **Documentation**: Complete technical documentation and manuals
