# NSW Procurement Platform - Integrated Compliance System

## 🎯 **System Overview**

The NSW Procurement Platform now features a **Unified Compliance Engine** that strongly integrates RAG, NSW Local Government Global Compliance, Blacktown City Council Standards, and AI intervention throughout all procurement processes. This creates a seamless, compliant, and intelligent procurement workflow.

## 🔗 **Strongly Integrated Components**

### 1. **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) System**
- **Blacktown RAG System**: Stores official Blacktown City Council evaluation standards
- **Global Compliance RAG**: Manages NSW Local Government universal standards
- **Unified RAG System**: Optimizes all compliance documents for intelligent retrieval
- **AI Intervention**: Real-time compliance checking and document analysis

### 2. **NSW Local Government Global Compliance**
- **Universal Standards**: Applied to all project types
- **Evaluation Templates**: Standard NSW procurement evaluation criteria
- **Compliance Validation**: Real-time verification against NSW guidelines
- **Document Integration**: Automatic compliance checking in all generated documents

### 3. **Blacktown City Council Standards**
- **Official Matrices**: Table 1-4 evaluation criteria and rating scales
- **Local Preference Policy**: 5% discount for Blacktown suppliers, 2.5% for Western Sydney
- **Social Procurement Guidelines**: Employment, training, and community benefits
- **Specific Compliance**: Tailored requirements for Blacktown projects

### 4. **AI Intervention & Automation**
- **Smart Procurement Flow**: AI determines optimal procurement process
- **Real-time Compliance**: Continuous compliance checking throughout workflow
- **Intelligent Evaluation**: AI-powered supplier scoring with multiple criteria
- **Document Generation**: AI creates compliant TEPP and returnable schedules
- **Risk Assessment**: AI analyzes and mitigates procurement risks
