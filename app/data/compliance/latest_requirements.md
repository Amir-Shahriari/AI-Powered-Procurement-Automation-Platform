# NSW Local Government Procurement Compliance Requirements 2024

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive compliance requirements for NSW Local Government procurement processes, ensuring adherence to all relevant legislation, guidelines, and standards.

## Legislative Framework

### Primary Legislation
1. **Local Government Act 1993**
   - Section 55: Tendering Requirements
   - Section 55A: Tender Evaluation Criteria
   - Section 55B: Exemptions from Tendering

2. **Work Health and Safety Act 2011**
   - Section 19: Primary Duty of Care
   - Section 20: Duty of Persons Conducting Business
   - Section 21: Duty of Officers

3. **Privacy Act 1988**
   - Australian Privacy Principles
   - Data breach notification requirements

### Secondary Legislation
1. **Local Government (General) Regulation 2021**
   - Part 4: Procurement Procedures
   - Part 5: Contract Management

## Procurement Guidelines

### Tendering Guidelines for NSW Local Government
- **Probity**: All processes must be conducted with integrity and fairness
- **Transparency**: Processes must be open and accountable
- **Value for Money**: Best value outcome considering whole-of-life costs
- **Competition**: Fair and open competition where appropriate

### Evaluation Criteria Standards
- **Price (Full Cost)**: 40-60% for construction, 30-50% for services
- **Relevant Experience**: 15-25% for construction, 20-30% for services
- **Capability**: 15-25% for construction, 20-30% for services
- **Management & Financial Capacity**: 10-20% for construction, 10-20% for services
- **Quality Management**: 5-15% for all categories
- **WHS**: 5-15% for all categories
- **Sustainability and EEO**: 5-15% for all categories

## Compliance Standards

### Work Health and Safety (WHS)
- **AS/NZS 4801:2001**: Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems
- **AS/NZS 45001:2018**: ISO 45001 certification preferred
- **WHS Management Plan**: Required for all construction projects
- **Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive risk assessment required

### Environmental Management
- **AS/NZS ISO 14001:2016**: Environmental Management Systems
- **Environmental Impact Assessment**: Required for significant projects
- **Waste Management Plans**: Required for construction projects
- **Sustainability Considerations**: Energy efficiency, water conservation

### Quality Management
- **AS/NZS ISO 9001:2016**: Quality Management Systems
- **Quality Assurance**: Documented quality processes
- **Performance Standards**: Clear performance criteria

### Australian Design Rules (ADR)
- **ADR 1/00**: Vehicle Standards
- **ADR 2/00**: Side Door Strength
- **ADR 3/00**: Seat Anchorages
- **ADR 4/00**: Seat Belt Anchorages
- **ADR 5/00**: Anchorages for Child Restraints

## Risk Management

### Risk Categories
1. **Financial Risk**: Supplier stability, payment terms, insurance
2. **Operational Risk**: Delivery timelines, quality standards, capacity
3. **Compliance Risk**: Regulatory compliance, WHS, environmental
4. **Reputational Risk**: Public perception, media attention, community impact

### Risk Assessment Process
1. Identify risks
2. Assess likelihood and impact
3. Develop mitigation strategies
4. Monitor and review

## Compliance Checklist

### Pre-Tender
- [ ] Needs analysis completed
- [ ] Budget approved
- [ ] Risk assessment conducted
- [ ] Evaluation criteria defined
- [ ] Probity advisor appointed (if required)
- [ ] Tender documentation prepared

### Tender Process
- [ ] Public notification published
- [ ] Tender period adequate
- [ ] Information sessions conducted (if required)
- [ ] Questions and answers documented
- [ ] Addenda issued (if required)
- [ ] Tender submissions received and secured

### Evaluation
- [ ] Evaluation panel appointed
- [ ] Evaluation criteria applied consistently
- [ ] Scoring methodology followed
- [ ] Documentation maintained
- [ ] Probity requirements met
- [ ] Evaluation report prepared

### Award
- [ ] Successful tenderer selected
- [ ] Contract negotiations completed
- [ ] Contract executed
- [ ] Unsuccessful tenderers notified
- [ ] Debriefing offered
- [ ] Records archived
