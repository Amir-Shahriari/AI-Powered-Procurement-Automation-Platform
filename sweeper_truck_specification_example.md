### PROJECT OVERVIEW
Blacktown City Council is seeking to procure 3 x heavy duty sweeper trucks to replace aging fleet vehicles. The vehicles will be used for street cleaning, maintenance of public areas, and emergency response cleaning operations across the Blacktown Local Government Area.

### SCOPE OF WORK
The successful tenderer shall supply and deliver 3 x heavy duty sweeper trucks that meet all specified requirements, including:

1. **Vehicle Supply**: 3 x new heavy duty sweeper trucks
2. **Delivery**: Delivery to Blacktown City Council depot
3. **Training**: Operator training for 6 personnel (2 per vehicle)
4. **Warranty**: 3-year comprehensive warranty
5. **Support**: 24-month parts and service support
6. **Documentation**: Complete technical documentation and manuals
